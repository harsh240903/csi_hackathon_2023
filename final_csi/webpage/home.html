Recent calamities
Joshimath declared ‘landslide-subsidence zone’

The number of families evacuated to temporary relief centres now stands at 68, Uttarakhand disaster management office said. Curelief volunteers showing great co-operation towards the aid of the victims

Magnitude earthquake struck Nepal

On the early morning of 9 November 2022, an earthquake of 6.6 magnitude struck Doti district in the far western part of Nepal. Several donations and care packages are sent from Curelief.

Assam under man-made floods

Assam’s Silchar town was submerged for 11 days by floodwaters from the Barak river, with water rising up to 12 feet (4 metres) in some places. Reinforcements and volunteers co-ordinated with the NDRF to evacuate the victims.

Chemical factory blast at Hapur

12 workers died and 13 were injured when a boiler exploded at a chemical factory in western Uttar Pradesh’s Hapur district. Curelief approached and helped in evacuation of the public in the affected area and provide health checkups and aid.
